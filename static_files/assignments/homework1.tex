  Equation 2.5 in the textbook (Understanding Deep Learning) gives the equation for linear regression with one input variable using the least-squares loss.
  \begin{eqnarray*}
    L[\phi] & = & \sum_{i=1}^{I} \left( f[x_i,\phi] - y_i \right)^2 \\
    & = & \sum_{i=1}^{I} \left( \phi_0 + \phi_1 x_i - y_i \right)^2
  \end{eqnarray*}

  \begin{enumerate}

  \item

    Derive the formula for \( \partial L / \partial \phi_0 \).

    \vspace{\stretch{1}}

  \item
    Derive the formula for \( \partial L / \partial \phi_1 \).

    \vspace{\stretch{1}}
  \end{enumerate}
